After (Improved Dark Theme)

Blog Post Title

This is how your blog content will look with the improved color scheme. The lighter backgrounds and better contrast make the text much more readable and comfortable for extended reading.

Article Card

Cards now use a semi-transparent background with backdrop blur for a modern, readable appearance.

Background: from gray-800 (#1f2937) to gray-900 (#111827)
Card: gray-700/50 with backdrop blur
Text: gray-300 (#d1d5db)
Headings: gray-100 (#f9fafb)
✓ Contrast ratio: ~7.5:1 (Passes WCAG AA & AAA)

Key Improvements

  • Better Contrast: Text is now more readable with improved contrast ratios that meet WCAG accessibility standards
  • Modern Design: Semi-transparent cards with backdrop blur create a sophisticated, layered appearance
  • Consistent Color System: Updated CSS variables ensure consistency across all dark mode elements
  • Enhanced Typography: Added Tailwind Typography plugin for better prose content styling
  • Subtle Gradients: Replaced harsh black backgrounds with subtle gray gradients for a softer look

Industry Best Practices Applied

  • Medium/Notion Style: Light gray backgrounds (#1f2937) instead of pure black for reduced eye strain
  • Stripe Design: High contrast text (#d1d5db on #1f2937) for optimal readability
  • Apple HIG: Subtle borders and shadows to create depth without harsh contrasts
  • Material Design 3: Proper elevation through opacity and blur effects
